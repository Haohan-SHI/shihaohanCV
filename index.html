Biography

Hi! I am an Assistant Professor at the School of Artificial Intelligence, Henan University, and a Research Collaborator at Loughborough University, working on AI Safety and communication security, with a focus on robust audio processing and audio deepfake detection in real-world communication scenarios. I have published in TASLP, ICASSP, and EUSIPCO, and have led or participated in research projects funded by the UK and China.
